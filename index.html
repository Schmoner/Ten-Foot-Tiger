Ten Foot Tiger is a Sacramento based collective of musicians driving solely by the desire to create stirring original music. With a sound beyond traditional categories, TFT serves up a wide variety that highlights funk, jazz, latin, pop, ballads, blues, electronic, rock and folk. There truly is a little something for everyone. You can expect poignant and provocative songs paired with powerful improvisations that stem from the many genres they cover. Dan Green has been a guitarist for over 20 years. His first band, Saturn Rising, began in South Lake Tahoe. He continued to play with Waiting For Tim, Adient Zoo, and Black Star Safari before creating Ten Foot Tiger.

Mike has been playing bass in the Sacramento music scene for decades. His other bands in the area include The Nibblers, Mubmo Gumbo, and more.

Billy is a Sacramento drummer with over 10 years of experience in the local music scene. His other bands include a The Gold Soulds, Boot Juice, and more.
